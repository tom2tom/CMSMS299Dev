Detail-Template Help

The News module's 'detail' action exports the following variables:

  • $category_link (string) - (deprecated) A link to a summary view for articles in the category
  • $category_name (string) - The internal-name of the category that the article belongs to
  • $entry (object) - a PHP object that contains information about one article
  • $return_url (string) - Page content (html) representing a link to the site-page specified for returning to after reading the article

$entry Object Properties

  • author (string) - The username of the article's creator
  • author_id (int) - The userid of the article's creator
  • authorname (string) - The full name of the article's creator. Only applicable if that information exists in the admin data.
  • canonical (string) - A full URL (prettified) to the article's detail view using defaults if necessary
  • category (string) - The name of the category that the article resides in (might be empty or unset)
  • category_id (int) - The unique id of the category that the article resides in (might be empty or unset)
  • enddate (string) - The date/time when the article should cease appearing on the site (might be empty or unset)
  • extra (string) - The "extra" data associated with the article (might be empty or unset)
  • id (int) - The unique article id
  • image_url (string) - The uploads-folder-relative URL of an associated image (might be empty or unset)
  • news_url (string) - URL-slug (pretty) for displaying a detail view of the article
  • returnid (string) - The id of the page to return to when finished reading the news-page
  • startdate (string) - The date/time when the article should begin to appear (might be empty or unset)
  • status (string) - Either 'draft' or 'published' indicating the status of the article
  • summary (string) - The article summary-text (might be empty or unset)
  • title (string) - The title of the article

Object members can be displayed by the following syntax: {$entry->membername} or assigned to a Smarty variable using {$foo = $entry->membername}.
