Browse-Category-Template Help

The News module's 'browsecat' action exports the following variables:

  • $cats (array) - An array of category information representing all categories in the News module
  • $count (int) - The number of members of cats

Category Information

Each member of the $cats array is itself an associative array.

The following are the notable members of such arrays. Other elements may also be present for internal organizational purposes.

  • alias (string) - A slug which may be included in a pretty-URL for linking to the article
  • count (int) - The number of valid displayable articles in the category
  • index (int) (deprecated) - An increasing index of the current category information. Instead use Smarty foreach-property @index.
  • image_url (string) - The uploads-folder-relative URL of an associated image (might be empty or unset)
  • item_order (int) - A numeric representation of the category's order among its peers
  • news_category_id (int) - The numeric id of the category
  • news_category_name (string) - The name of the category
  • parent_id (int) - The numeric id of the category's parent. A value less than one indicates no parent.
  • url (string) - URL for showing a summary-view of all valid displayable articles in the category

The category information may be used in a template like {$cats[0]['news_category_name']} or {$cats.0.news_category_name} etc.
