Approval-Email-Template Help

The News module exports the following variables for use in such templates:

  • $title (string) - The article title
  • $summary (string) - The article summary (might be empty)
  • $startdate (string) - The intended publication date/time (might be empty if ASAP is intended)
  • $enddate (string) - The intended take-down date/time (might be empty if manual takedown is intended)
